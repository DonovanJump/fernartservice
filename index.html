About Fern Art Services

Fern Art Services was founded on a simple belief: every work of art deserves the same careful attention it received when it was created. With over a decade of experience serving private collectors, galleries, auction houses, and museums, our team brings unmatched expertise to every project.

We are fully insured and trained in the latest art-handling techniques, ensuring that your collection is in the safest hands possible — from the moment we arrive to the moment we leave.
